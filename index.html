A short background about Akan names

Akan names originate from the Ghanian culture where children are given first names as the day of the week they were born. There are different names for boy and girls, hence the gender field.

Find out your Akan name in 3 simple steps

  1. Fill in your date of birth
  2. Choose your gender
  3. Click the submit button
And there you go!
